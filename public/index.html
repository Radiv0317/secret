Tojo-Kai | 東城会

The Tojo-Kai is a dominant Yakuza syndicate built on tradition, discipline, and absolute loyalty. Rising from the ashes of the Chihara-kai, the clan now enforces its rule with calculated brutality, strategic alliances, and a relentless pursuit of power.

Clan Structure

  • 女上司 | (Onna)-Kumicho — Clan Head
  • Saiko Komon — Chief Advisor
  • So-Honbuncho — Oversees Ops & Events
  • Wakagashira — Executive(s)
  • Shateigashira — Lieutenant(s)
  • Kyodai — Full Member
  • Shatei — Junior Member
  • Senshi — Aspirant
